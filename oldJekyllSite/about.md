Noah Waters is a violinist and violin teacher. Holding degrees from the University of Michigan (BM) and the University of Ottawa (MM), he focuses on performing solo and chamber music and maintains a varied concert schedule ranging from weddings and social functions to contemporary music concerts and pit orchestras. Noah has studied the violin with Yehonatan Berick and Solomia Soroka principally, and has played under the baton of Kenneth Kiesler, David Currie, Rennie Regehr, Elim Chan, Alain Trudel and many others.
